About Twin Shore Stays

Our Story

About Us

Welcome to Twin Shore Stays! We're Jackson and Liz, the husband-and-wife team behind this boutique vacation rental management company based in San Diego, CA.

After moving from Los Angeles to San Diego to raise our twin daughters, we combined our skills and passions to create Twin Shore Stays—a company focused on delivering exceptional experiences for guests and property owners alike.

Jackson has always had a passion for real estate and dreamed of owning a business. With a background in project management and engineering, he excels at optimizing operations and implementing efficient systems. Liz brings an eye for design and creativity, along with the compassion she cultivated during her career in emergency medicine.

Together, we're a strong team: Jackson develops the vision for our company, and Liz helps bring it to life. At Twin Shore Stays, our mission is to create welcoming spaces that feel like home while helping property owners get the most out of their investments.

Whether you're a guest seeking a great stay or a homeowner looking for expert management, we're here to make it happen.
